A quick visualisation of the poistion of the geographic poles of the earth, which constitute the axis around which the earth rotates
and the geomagnetic poles of the earth, which are poles of an approximation of the earth's magnetic field as a bar magnet.

Values taken from the Wiki:

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geomagnetic_pole

The creation of this visualisation was prompted by the images from the ARTEMIS 2 mission which led to a discussion around auroral ovals
being visible and being a rough approximation of the positions of the geomagnetic poles.
https://x.com/neel_kolhe/status/2040091370635870601

This was made to present a more realistic visualisation of the 11 degree offset between the axes.
